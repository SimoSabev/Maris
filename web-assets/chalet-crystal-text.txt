About
the
Chalet
An ultra-luxurious retreat in Courchevel 1850,
Chalet Crystal sleeps up to 15 guests in 8 en-suite
bedrooms. 
Blending contemporary elegance with alpine
charm, the chalet features grand living and dining
areas, a stylish bar and wine cave, and floor-to-
ceiling windows with mountain views. The private
spa is a true highlight, with an infinity pool,
Jacuzzi, Hammam, massage room and outdoor hot
tub. 
Guests can also enjoy a cinema room, games room,
and ski-in convenience, making Chalet Crystal the
perfect balance of relaxation and entertainment.
