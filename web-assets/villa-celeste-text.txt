About
the Villa
Villa Celeste is an ultra-luxury waterfront villa
in Aleomandra, Mykonos, offering
breathtaking views over the Aegean Sea and
nearby islands.
Set across three levels, it features 6 elegant
en-suite bedrooms, spacious living areas, and
two fully equipped kitchens, all designed with
a modern and refined aesthetic.
Outdoors, guests can enjoy a private infinity
pool, fire pit, and a master suite terrace with
jacuzzi, creating an exceptional setting for
sunsets, sea views, and a truly exclusive island
stay.
