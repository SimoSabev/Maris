About
the Villa
Villa Metis is a striking 10-bedroom Cycladic villa in
Mykonos, designed to host up to 20 guests in
complete privacy across five expansive levels. 
Blending refined luxury with traditional Greek
architectural elements, the villa offers elegant
interiors, all en-suite bedrooms, and panoramic views
over the Aegean Sea from private terraces. A
separate guest house with its own entrance adds
flexibility, while outdoor living takes centre stage with
a heated infinity pool, jacuzzi, pool bar, BBQ and
multiple lounge areas overlooking the coastline. 
Villa Metis is an exceptional retreat for large groups
seeking space, style and uninterrupted sea views.
