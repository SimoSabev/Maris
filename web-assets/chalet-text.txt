About
the
Chalet
Set in prestigious Courchevel 1850, Chalet Nordéa
spans 837 m² over five levels and sleeps 14 guests
in six en-suite bedrooms plus a children’s bunk
room. 
The grand vaulted living space opens to a wrap-
around balcony and stylish dining area with open
kitchen. A remarkable private spa includes a pool,
Hammam, sauna, massage room, Nordic bath and
outdoor barrel sauna with hot tub, while a games
lounge with bar, billiards and air hockey keeps
everyone entertained. 
An elevator and ski room complete this standout
ski-in/ski-out retreat.
